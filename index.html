Oi! Hi there!

I'm Tuff! I'm a 19 year old female human from Sweden. The two things I love above everything else in this world is my cat, μ (from the greek letter Mu. It makes sense in Swedish) and creating.




I spend my days at an IT-company, developing iOS apps for scandinavian forest owners. But, when the weekend comes around, I get my art supplies out and I let my hands go wild. 

People usually find it funny how my interests seem so far apart, but when I say creating, I mean all forms. Writing down code that then generates a fully functioning program gives me the same satisfaction as having an idea of an art piece and then making that come to life on a paper or canvas. 

I draw a bit of everything and try a lof of different materials, searching to improve my technique and expand my artistry beyond what is currently comfortable. However, I will probably always like making graphite portraits the most. My first true love.

As many other young adults, I still struggle with finding my purpose - my guiding compass in life. But, I am a problem solver, so I layed down the facts and decided what to keep as a goal for at least the upcoming few years. Next year I want to attend a university. I don't know if it's going to be technical or artistic, nor in which city! But the #1 priority is that I will be able to continue creating throughout my university education with μ by my side. That is my goal.
